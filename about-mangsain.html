Mang Sain

Andhika Syaputra • Software Developer

Mang Sain, whose formal name is Andhika Syaputra, is an Indonesian software developer with a deep cultural heritage rooted in the Semende community of South Sumatra. Raised in Lampung and descended from the revered Puyang Cili Anom of Merlawai, Muara Enim, he proudly represents the values of his ancestors in today’s digital era.

With a technical background and academic foundation from Universitas Aisyah Pringsewu, Mang Sain specializes in desktop and mobile development using PowerBuilder, Android Studio, and cloud-integrated databases such as Firebase and SQL Server. His current focus includes digital privacy, data protection, and building applications that respect ethical digital standards.

As a cultural ambassador and technology advocate, he merges tradition with innovation. Through his GitHub Page, Mang Sain shares his journey, not only as a developer but as a voice from the Semende generation rising in the digital world.

“I ask not just with words, but with intent: ‘Dek tau ngilok’i, jangan merusak jadilah’ — because we walk together in code, in culture, and in spirit.”
